V¹ vs VX (Overview)

Spoiler-light. A quick primer on how readers should think about the two terms.

TL;DR

V¹ = primordial Void residue tied to consciousness and memory. VX = modern, reactive matter we can manipulate.

In‑story

Soldiers talk about V¹ like a ghost that lingers in places and people. Engineers treat VX like material — alloyed, shaped, embedded. They interact, but they are not the same.

Reader notes

  • V¹ shows up in lore/mystery moments.
  • VX shows up in tech, weapons, and infrastructure.

Deeper details live in the Archive.
