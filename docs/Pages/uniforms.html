Uniforms

Codex Fragment – D.S.O. Uniform Protocols

Classification: Fieldwear Regulation

Clearance: Class D

Initiates (F to C-Rank)

Trainees are issued a plain black military set—pressed jacket and slacks, minimalist in design, resembling formalwear.

Each uniform is embedded with a low-grade Sigil of Protection, offering baseline Void resistance and environmental shielding.

Agents (B to A-Rank)

Field-ready personnel are granted a revised set: black military attire with a short leather coat, worn draped over the shoulders in cloak-like fashion.

This design prioritizes mobility and symbolic presence.

Elite Operatives (S-Rank and above)

Those who stand at the apex of the agency receive the Helm of Authority—a high-ranking insignia signifying command status and operational sovereignty.

It is the second-highest honor bestowed within the D.S.O.

Note: Uniform adaptations may vary under special assignment or regional command.
