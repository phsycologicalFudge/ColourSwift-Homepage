[Codex Fragment: The D.S.O — Demonic Surveillance Organization]

Founded in the fifth century A.W. (After War), the Demonic Surveillance Organization, or D.S.O, was established to combat the rising tide of night-born demonic entities. What began as a desperate defense initiative evolved into humanity’s strongest bulwark—the iron wall between extinction and survival. Today, it stands not just as a military power, but as a symbol of discipline, fear, and salvation.

❖ D.S.O Rank Structure ❖

Ranks are divided into two primary operational classes: Guard Ranks and Agent Ranks, with the rarest title—Holy Pillar—standing above all.

Guard Ranks (Frontline Initiation and Defense Units)

  • F-Rank – Trainees undergoing foundational combat and Void exposure protocols.
  • E-Rank – Exam-ready candidates marked for field evaluation.
  • D-Rank – Fully enlisted Common Ground Soldiers (C.G.S.); assigned basic field duties.
  • C-Rank – Senior C.G.S.; experienced operatives specializing in support, reconnaissance, or logistics.

Agent Ranks (Elite Operatives and High-Level Combat Units)

  • B-Rank – Certified field agents with stable Void reserves; capable of limited demon suppression missions.
  • A-Rank – High-level tacticians and combatants; often deployed solo to decimate enemy strongholds.
  • S-Rank – The apex of standard ranks. Their Void resonance and power output are often compared to natural disasters.

Holy Pillar (Transcendent Authority)

More than a title—it is a legacy carved in blood, silence, and unshakable will.

Holy Pillars are not merely warriors. They are the embodiment of the D.S.O.’s soul.

They are said to carry within them the truths of existence:

  • Life.
  • Death.
  • Joy.
  • Pain.

Their very presence is said to bend the atmosphere.
Even the heavens lean beneath a Pillar’s will.
