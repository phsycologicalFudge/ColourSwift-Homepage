Codex Fragment – Elemental Movement Arts

Classification: Controlled Combat Applications

Clearance: Class C (Restricted to Voidwalker Personnel)

Compiled by: Command Division, Sector 3 Archives

Elemental Movement Arts are not magic spells.

They are kinetic combat systems — rooted in VX-neuromuscular flow, refined by breath, and shaped through repetition.

Only a small number of Void Walkers possess the neural alignment required to activate even the basic sequences of each art.

As such, they are classified as non-essential augmentations and are not a requirement for standard D.S.O. field operations.

Note: Improper execution of any Art may result in severe muscle tissue atrophy (degradation).

Thunder Arts

Lightning, through muscle.

  • Enables sharp bursts of speed and violent directional shifts via VX-pathways.
  • Appears as teleportation to untrained eyes.
  • Used for evasion or close-quarter assault.
  • Side effects: blurred vision, blood vessel rupture, disorientation.
  • Master Form: REDACTED

Water Arts

Flow, not force.

  • Mimics water currents — adaptive, redirectional, and patient.
  • Disrupts rhythm through feints and missteps.
  • Ideal for counter-based or attrition strategies.
  • Rarely lethal, but enhances survivability.
  • Only one offensive user recorded in D.S.O. history.

Sound Arts

Every step is an echo.

  • Manipulates vibrations and rhythm to distort perception and focus.
  • Functions as soft crowd control.
  • Can operate without visible movement when mastered.
  • Requires deep internal silence.

Music Arts Restricted Class: Composer Type

An evolution of Sound Arts.

  • Generates harmonic loops that warp time and local reality.
  • Only two confirmed users in D.S.O. history.
  • Synchronizes with VX-fields to alter battlefield behavior.
  • Not viable until full resonance is achieved.
