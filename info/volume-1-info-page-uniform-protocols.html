(Volume 1) Info Page: Uniform Protocols

Basic Uniform Protocols

The D.S.O requires all agents to wear a basic black military uniform. A cross-like sigil of

protection is placed on the lapel, along with a pair of black boots that are issued to all

soldiers.

  • Helm of Authority - Reserved for the Holy Pillars.
