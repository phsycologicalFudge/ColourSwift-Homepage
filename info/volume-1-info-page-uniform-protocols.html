(Volume 1) Info Page: Uniform Protocols

Basic Uniform Protocols

The D.S.O requires all agents to wear a basic black military uniform. A cross -like sigil of protection is placed on the lapel, along with a pair of black boots that are issued to all soldiers.

Additional Uniform Protocols

  • • Cape - Agents of higher ranks may be rewarded with a fashionable cape.
  • Helm of Authority - Reserved for the Holy Pillars.
