(Volume 2) Info Page: World Sectors & Outposts

Sectors

During the initial reconstruction of the Gaia, formally known as Earth before the war,

the World Government assigned the remaining countries a numerical identifier for

simple communication.

Examples

  • Sector 9 = Japan
  • Sector 1 = United Kingdom
  • Sector 5 = United States
  • Sector 8 = China

Cardinal Directions

To identify individual cities within a sector, a directional tag was added

  • A = North
  • B = East
  • C = South
  • D = West

This creates combined identifiers

  • Sector 1C = United Kingdom, South (London)
  • Sector 9B = Japan, east (New Tokyo)

Outposts

Each directional tag is further subdivided into smaller units called outposts.

For example, the city of Blackreach is assigned the numerical value 7, with the

directional tag A (North). Following this logic

  • Outpost 7A, Sector 9B is located in East Japan, north of New Tokyo.
