Void Pressure

“Weight on the soul, not the body.” How characters experience it, without spoilers.

What it feels like

  • Suffocation without breath, drowning without water.
  • Nothing in the environment moves; only living things feel it.

How to read it

If a scene goes silent, or people collapse without visible force, assume a strong Void presence is at work.

Mechanics and edge cases are expanded inside the Archive.
