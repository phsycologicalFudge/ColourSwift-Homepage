(Volume 2) Info page: The L.E.P

Founded by the World Government two centuries after the Last War, the Law Enforcement Police (L.E.P) was created as a global force tasked with upholding the law and ensuring public safety worldwide.

Initially, each Sector had its own politics and police force. Over time, tensions rose and an international conflict loomed. This led to a historical event known as ‘The Hypocrits’, where every political group was disbanded and new factions were formed aligned with the World Government’s vision.
