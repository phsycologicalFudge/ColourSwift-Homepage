What is The Void?

Beyond the stars, beyond the gods who rule them, exists a dimension. One of infinite

possibilities. The void is not a mere dimension within existence, but a living, primordial

entity created by the Architect. Some say it is a direct part of the Architect. Others say it

is one of the first beings created by the Architect. Nobody knows.

The only thing we truly understand about The Void is that it chooses.
