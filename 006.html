Exam

[Codex Fragment: The D.S.O. Entrance Exam]

The Demonic Surveillance Organization (D.S.O.) requires all potential agents to undergo a three-stage qualification process designed to filter, forge, and finalize the warriors of humanity’s last line of defense.

Those who pass the exam are officially recognized as Voidguards or D.S.O. Agents, and are granted a rank reflective of their performance—ranging from D to S.
