Codex Fragment: The Emphetripia Heart

After the final war against the Voidborn, the world changed.

The Emphetripia Gland—an unnatural second heart—grew beneath the first.

It did not beat. It stored. Void. A reservoir of unshaped power.

The larger the gland, the greater the potential. It became the foundation of humanity's Void Walking.

The Tenshiro were said to be blessed. Born with greater hearts. Greater futures. Or so the world believed.
