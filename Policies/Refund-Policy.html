Refund Policy

Last updated March 6, 2026

1. Overview

This Refund Policy explains how refunds are handled for purchases of AvarionX Premium and any other subscription products offered by Colourswift Limited.

This policy applies to purchases made through our authorised payment providers, including Lemon Squeezy where used.

2. Definitions

For the purposes of this Refund Policy:

  • "Company", "we", "us", or "our" means Colourswift Limited.
  • "Customer", "you", or "your" means the person who purchased the subscription.
  • "Subscription" means a recurring billing product that provides access to paid features for a defined billing period.
  • "Initial purchase" means the first paid transaction for a subscription by a customer for that product.
  • "Renewal" means any subsequent recurring payment after the initial purchase.
  • "Heavily used" means usage that materially exceeds normal evaluation or trial use, including unusually high bandwidth consumption, repeated use across multiple devices, or patterns consistent with abuse or automated usage.

3. Payment Processing

Payments are processed by third-party payment providers. Where Lemon Squeezy is used, Lemon Squeezy acts as the merchant of record for the transaction and handles payment processing and tax collection where applicable.

4. Initial Purchase Refunds

You may request a refund within 14 days of the initial purchase of a subscription, provided the service has not been heavily used.

Refund requests should be submitted through the payment provider used for the purchase or by contacting support@colourswift.com with your order details.

If a refund is approved, access to the subscription may be revoked and the subscription may be cancelled to prevent further billing.

5. Renewals and Billing Periods

Renewal charges are generally non-refundable once processed, except where required by applicable law.

You may cancel a subscription at any time to prevent future renewals. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period unless otherwise stated at the time of purchase.

6. Abuse, Heavy Usage, and Ineligibility

We may deny a refund where usage indicates the service has been heavily used or where there is evidence of misuse. This may include:

  • High bandwidth consumption inconsistent with evaluation use.
  • Repeated use across multiple devices or frequent connection cycling in a short period.
  • Automated usage, credential sharing, or attempts to bypass plan limits.
  • Activity that violates our Terms of Use.

7. Refund Method and Processing Times

Approved refunds are issued through the original payment method via the payment provider. Processing times depend on the payment provider and your financial institution.

We do not control bank processing times after a refund has been initiated by the payment provider.

8. Chargebacks and Payment Disputes

If you have an issue with billing or service access, contact support@colourswift.com before initiating a chargeback. Chargebacks may result in immediate suspension of the subscription and related accounts while the dispute is investigated.

9. Statutory Rights

Nothing in this Refund Policy limits any rights you may have under applicable consumer protection laws. Where you have statutory rights that apply to digital services or subscriptions, those rights will apply in addition to this policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
