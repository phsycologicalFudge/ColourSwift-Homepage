Privacy Policy

Last updated December 30, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Notice for Colourswift Limited (“we,” “us,” or “our”) explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services (“Services”), including our website, mobile applications, and cloud services.

If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please discontinue use of our Services. If you have questions, contact us at support@colourswift.com.

1.1 Disclaimer for ColourSwift Security

ColourSwift Security provides antivirus protection through a combination of on-device scanning and cloud-assisted threat intelligence. All file analysis and scanning logic execute locally on device. No file contents, metadata, or scan results are uploaded to or stored on ColourSwift servers.

When enabled, realtime protection uses cloud-based threat intelligence to improve detection accuracy. This process involves submitting cryptographic file hashes for threat lookup purposes only. Hash lookups do not include file contents and are not used for profiling, analytics, or tracking.

Manual scans may be performed without using cloud-based threat intelligence. When cloud lookups are disabled for manual scanning, all detection is performed entirely on-device.

The app may request permissions such as storage access or notification access solely to enable local scanning and realtime protection. These permissions are never used for analytics, marketing, profiling, or advertising.

This disclaimer applies specifically to ColourSwift Security. Other ColourSwift Limited services follow the broader privacy principles outlined in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and maintain our services. This includes:

When using offline ColourSwift applications:

  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers.
  • All files and activity remain stored locally on your device.
  • Storage access is requested only to enable local functionality.

When using cloud-based threat lookup services:

  • Cryptographic file hashes submitted for malware detection purposes.
  • No file contents are uploaded.
  • Hashes are used solely to perform threat intelligence lookups.
  • Hashes are not used for profiling, analytics, or advertising.

When using ColourSwift Cloud Storage services or creating an account:

  • Account details such as name, email, username, and password.
  • Files and other content you choose to upload to cloud storage services.
  • Payment data processed securely by third-party providers. We do not store full card details.
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device identifiers for security and diagnostic purposes.

We do not collect location data, contact lists, behavioural tracking data, or advertising profiles.

Account-related data is retained for as long as your account remains active. Upon account deletion, personal data is deleted or anonymised unless retention is required by law.

Where no personal data is collected or stored, retention periods do not apply.

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational safeguards to protect personal information. Communications with cloud services are encrypted using modern industry standards.

Offline ColourSwift applications operate entirely locally unless cloud features are explicitly enabled.

While no online system can guarantee absolute security, our design philosophy prioritises user privacy and data minimisation.

7. Minors

Our services are not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children below the applicable age of digital consent under local law.

Where required by applicable law, a child may be defined as a person under 18 years of age. If we become aware that personal data relating to a child has been collected, such data will be deleted.

8. Your Rights

Under UK and EU data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion.
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.

You may exercise these rights by contacting support@colourswift.com.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner’s Office or your local data protection authority.

8.1 India (Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023)

For users located in India, ColourSwift processes personal data in accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act).

Under the DPDP Act, Indian users are considered “Data Principals” and have the right to:

  • Access a summary of their personal data.
  • Request correction or erasure of personal data.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.
  • Raise grievances related to the processing of their personal data.

Requests and grievances under the DPDP Act may be submitted using the same contact details provided in this policy. The support email listed in the Contact section also serves as the grievance redressal contact for data-protection related concerns.

Personal data may be processed outside India where required to provide the Services. Such processing is carried out subject to appropriate safeguards and applicable law.

Personal data relating to Indian users is erased once the specified purpose is fulfilled or consent is withdrawn, unless retention is required by law.
