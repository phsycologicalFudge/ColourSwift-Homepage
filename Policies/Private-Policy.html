Privacy Policy

Last updated February 22, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Notice for Colourswift Limited explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services, including our website, mobile applications, VPN services, DNS filtering services, and cloud services.

If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please discontinue use of our Services. If you have questions, contact support@colourswift.com.

1.1 Disclaimer for ColourSwift Security/AvarionX Antivirus

AvarionX Antivirus provides antivirus protection through a combination of on-device scanning and cloud-assisted threat intelligence. All file analysis and scanning logic execute locally on device. No file contents, metadata, or scan results are uploaded to or stored on ColourSwift servers.

When enabled, realtime protection may submit cryptographic file hashes for threat lookup purposes only. Hash lookups do not include file contents and are not used for profiling, analytics, or advertising.

Manual scans may be performed without cloud lookups. When cloud lookups are disabled, all detection is performed entirely on-device.

Permissions such as storage or notification access are requested solely to enable local scanning and realtime protection.

1.2 VX-Link (Secure VPN and DNS Filtering)

VX-Link is the network protection component of ColourSwift. It includes Secure VPN and DNS Filtering functionality.

Secure VPN routes your device traffic through our VPN servers using modern encryption protocols. While connected, your public IP address is replaced with the IP address of the selected server region.

DNS Filtering routes DNS queries through our filtering infrastructure to block known malware domains, advertisements, trackers, and other unwanted categories. DNS Filtering may be used independently or together with Secure VPN.

We do not retain browsing history. We do not retain DNS query history linked to individual users. DNS queries are processed in transit solely for the purpose of applying filtering rules and returning a response.

To operate the service, validate subscriptions, enforce plan limits, prevent abuse, and maintain reliability, we may process limited technical data including IP address, selected server region, connection status, aggregate bandwidth usage totals, and device identifiers. This data is used strictly for service functionality, security, and account management purposes.

We may generate limited diagnostic and operational logs necessary to maintain, secure, and improve the service. Such logs are not designed to create or retain records of individual browsing activity.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and maintain our services.

Offline applications

  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers.
  • All files and activity remain stored locally on your device.

Cloud-based threat lookup

  • Cryptographic file hashes for malware detection.
  • No file contents uploaded.

Secure VPN and DNS Filtering

  • Selected server region and connection status.

  • DNS queries processed in transit for filtering purposes, not stored as per-user history.

Accounts and Cloud Storage

  • Files uploaded to cloud storage services.

  • Technical data such as IP address and device identifiers for diagnostics.

We do not collect precise location data, contact lists, behavioural tracking data, or advertising profiles.
